Deforestation in Argentina

Preserving our forests, protecting the future

What is Deforestation?

Deforestation is the loss of forest areas due to human activities such as intensive agriculture, livestock farming, and indiscriminate logging.

Situation in Argentina

Argentina has lost millions of hectares of native forests, especially in the Gran Chaco region, due to agricultural expansion and lack of effective controls.

Environmental Impacts

Deforestation causes biodiversity loss, increases greenhouse gas emissions, and disrupts hydrological cycles, affecting climate and local communities.

Forest Fires in Argentina

Fires are one of the main causes of forest loss and accelerate the deforestation process in vulnerable regions.
